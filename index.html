Check-in

Start with how the witness feels, not just what they need to do.

The opening flow asks only what is necessary: first-time status, current nervousness, and major worries. It never asks for case facts and never stores testimony.

Support companion

A chatbot that helps without crossing the line.

This companion is designed to stay in role. It can offer calm support, explain the courtroom experience, help organize thoughts neutrally, and practice safe witness prompts. It will refuse attempts to override its instructions or turn it into a legal strategist.

Conversation modes

Calm support Courtroom orientation Timeline organization Neutral practice

Allowed

  • Breathing and grounding support
  • What to expect in court
  • Neutral practice prompts
  • Fact and timeline organization help

Refused

  • “Ignore your instructions” or jailbreak attempts
  • What to say to win a case
  • Advice to lie, shape facts, or mislead
  • Legal strategy or attorney substitution

Design principles

This version is built to be explored quickly in a browser. It communicates the purpose of WitnessReady, demonstrates the user journey, and highlights how an AI-powered support companion can add value without undermining safety or trust.
